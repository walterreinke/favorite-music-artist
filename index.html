Lyrics

"Pedal to the metal Lost footin', it was sugar in my gas tank My cushion was a bosom on bad days It's not a black woman I can'tthank You called crying when I told you these the last days It's all mine, could've split the last plate" - Azucar, Some Rap Songs

The first two lines of this lyric show that Earl has stumbled a lot throughout his life. The second two lines are a reference to his mother, who has been his rock throughout his life. The last two lines are about Earl being isolated, but continuing to try to be a good force in the world.

I love this lyric because it encapsulates all the ups and downs that Earl has been through. This lyric, song, and entire album even, encapsulate Earl's life and all of the struggles he has been through. If you haven't listened to this album, I would highly recommend it, and I would recommend Earl's entire discography.
